About the college


St. Andrew Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM) is located in Gurgaon, Haryana, India. The institute is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management is situated in the industrial hub of Gurgaon, Delhi (NCR), India. SAITM Gurgaon benefits from its strategic location and accessibility. The campus is spread across 22 acres of lush green surroundings and boasts a globally acknowledged infrastructure. It is equipped with modern facilities including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, seminar halls, sports facilities, and hostel accommodation for students. The classrooms are centrally air-conditioned and equipped with modern teaching technology.
